ABOUT US

TASA @ UC Davis is a cultural and social student organization which serves as a platform for Thai students to connect. We as a Thai community thrive to bring people closer within our Community! We host events and activities for TASA members to get together and socialize. We welcome all Thai-American students that wishes to pursue a higher education in University of California, Davis.
